OUR STORY

Who we are, and why we are here.

Artificial Intelligence is changing the world faster than schools can adapt. AI can write essays, debug code, and analyze financial markets. But AI cannot empathize, think critically under ethical pressure, lead a team, or build deep connections.

The Unmachined was founded by a Class 12 student in India who realized that the most important skills for our future are the ones that make us uniquely human. This is not a passive webinar. It is a monthly training ground for teens who want to remain irreplaceable.

We connect with top minds from leading companies and organisations, not to lecture you, but to engage in small-group interactive challenges where you learn by doing.

Mohammedayan Khan

Founder & Organizer Class 12 Student · India
"AI is rewriting the rules of the future. We want to make sure teenagers are writing their own. We don't need lectures; we need guidance on how to navigate the human side of technology."

How it works

1

Participant applies

Apply for the session. Only 4 active participants are chosen per month for peak interactive dialogue, but others can audit.

2

Pre-work sent

20 days before the live session, you receive a package containing curated readings, prompts, and a challenge scenario. Submit your work one week prior to the session date.

3

Live 45-min session

An intensive online discussion. No slides. You pitch your solutions directly to the guest industry expert and receive real feedback.

4

Reflection submitted

Submit a short post-session reflection and receive a digital participation token.
